Industry Experience

Currently I work on the backend connectivity architecture for medical infusion devices at Becton Dickinson. We are a small team dedicated to providing a low-latency, high traffic volume connection to a dedicated server. As the most senior dev on the connectivity team, I also take on the role of tech lead and subject-matter expert. What this looks like is I spend a good majority of my day supporting troubleshooting and debugging issue with my colleagues.
